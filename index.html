Welcome to La Petite Académie

La Petite Académie is a Montessori daycare dedicated to fostering independence, creativity, and a love for learning in children aged 18 months to 5 years. Located in Montreal, our child-centered environment encourages exploration and growth through hands-on activities and personalized care.

Our Montessori Approach

Hands-On Learning

Children engage with Montessori materials to develop critical thinking and motor skills.

Independence

Our environment empowers children to make choices and build confidence.

Creative Play

Art, music, and imaginative play spark creativity and joy in learning.
